Me

Currently a full-time MechE and EECS (Electrical Engineering and Computer Science) student at MIT, I'm also a part-time writer, entreprenuer, pianist, runner, pâtissier, and comedian. I like to make stuff, whether it be poetry, cake, code, molecules, bots, music, or art.

In one word, I'm quirky, as demonstrated by
* this poem which may or may not accurately describes my state of being as a summer intern at a pharmaceutical/cosmetic company in France
* the fact that I want a snake as a pet
* the fact that I'm a hippie vegan wearing only second-hand clothes while living in a clothing-optional co-op
* and some interesting pictures of me on Facebook...

HOWEVER, no person can be summed up in one word. So I encourage you to actually get to know me by reading what I write and following me on LinkedIn, Facebook, and Github!
